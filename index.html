About Me

I bridge interdisciplinary research and technical project leadership, holding an MA in Digital Humanities (Stuttgart) and a BA in Interdisciplinary American Studies (Tübingen). Certified in project management (CAPM®, Google PM) and agile frameworks (PSM I), I specialize in coordinating IT and digitalization projects—from prototyping and user-centered design to managing software development workflows.

My technical skills include::

  • Python, NLP, data analysis
  • SQL/NoSQL databases
  • UX research & prototyping (Figma, Twine), informed by hands-on game development experience
  • Jira, Scrum/Kanban

I bring strong analytical and communication skills, with experience in cross-functional collaboration and translating stakeholder needs into structured solutions. My background in game development enhances my user research approach, providing practical insights into interactive systems and user engagement. Check out my games down below if you like!

Open to roles in IT coordination, digital project management, or research-oriented positions—particularly within public, cultural, or tech-driven organizations.

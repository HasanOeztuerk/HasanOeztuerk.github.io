About Me

Welcome! My name is Hasan C. Öztürk. I currently live in Germany and am deeply interested in all aspects of game development and design.

I studied Interdisciplinary American Studies (BA) at the University of Tübingen and recently completed my Digital Humanities (MA) at the University of Stuttgart, both with honors. My academic work focused on multivariate narration and different approaches to narrative design in games—particularly how environmental storytelling, player agency, and flow states shape interactive experiences.

In my free time, I write stories for RPGs, design quests, items, and monsters, and play tabletop role-playing games. As an avid gamer, I pay close attention to how games are constructed and what makes them engaging.

I'm currently developing my technical skills in Unity and Lua, while also working toward certification as a SCRUM Master to strengthen my project management capabilities in game development.
